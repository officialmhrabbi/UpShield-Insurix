Buy Car Insurance policies in Bangladesh online at UpShield Insurix. Get your customized price quote and order online quickly using the UpShield Insurix Website or Mobile App. Motor Insurance covers your vehicle against accidental damage, theft, fire, and third-party liability for injury or property damage.

Every registered motor vehicle in Bangladesh is legally required to carry at least Motor Liability (Third Party) coverage under the Motor Vehicles Ordinance. UpShield Insurix lets you compare policies from top insurers, get an instant digital cover note, and enjoy free doorstep delivery of your policy certificate anywhere in Bangladesh.

Comprehensive (Full) Coverage protects your vehicle against own damage (accident, fire, theft, natural disaster) as well as third-party liability for injury or property damage caused by your vehicle.

Motor Liability (Third Party) Coverage Only is the minimum legally required cover in Bangladesh. It pays for injury or damage you cause to others but does not cover damage to your own vehicle.

If your car is new, financed, or high in value, Comprehensive Coverage is recommended so you are protected against repair or replacement costs. If your vehicle is older and you mainly want to meet the legal requirement at the lowest cost, Motor Liability Coverage Only may be sufficient. Compare quotes from multiple insurers on UpShield Insurix to find the best price for the coverage you need.

Car insurance premiums depend on the vehicle's engine capacity (CC), market value, age, usage (private or commercial), and the coverage type selected. Comprehensive policies cost more than Liability-only policies since they cover a wider range of risks. Use the UpShield Insurix platform to instantly compare premiums from multiple insurers.

Common exclusions include damage from driving without a valid license, driving under the influence, wear and tear, mechanical or electrical breakdown, use of the vehicle outside the permitted purpose (e.g. private car used commercially), and war or nuclear risks. Always review the policy document for the full list of exclusions before purchasing.

In case of an accident, theft, or damage, inform the insurer's helpline as soon as possible and, if required, file a General Diary (GD) with the nearest police station. Gather supporting documents such as photos of the damage, the police report, your registration and license copies, and repair estimates. Submit the claim form along with these documents — UpShield Insurix's claims team assists you throughout the process until settlement.

Claims are settled as per the terms of the policy. For Comprehensive policies, own-damage claims are settled on a repair or cash-in-lieu basis up to the insured value, subject to any applicable deductible and depreciation on parts. Third-party liability claims are settled as per the liability determined and the coverage limits stated in the policy.
