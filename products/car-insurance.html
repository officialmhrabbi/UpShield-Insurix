You can buy motor car insurance online easily using UpShield Insurix website and mobile app. UpShield Insurix offers car insurance policy from the most reputed insurance companies who are committed to ensure financial protection in case your car gets damaged or you cause a damage to someone else while driving the car in the streets. You can pay the insurance premium using any bank card or mobile financial services as per your convenience.

There are two main types of car insurance: Comprehensive (Full) Coverage which covers vehicle damage, theft, and third-party liability; and Motor Liability Coverage Only which covers third-party liability at a lower premium. Comprehensive coverage is recommended for newer vehicles.

If your car is relatively new or high-value, Comprehensive coverage is recommended as it protects against theft, accidents, and natural disasters. For older vehicles, Motor Liability Coverage may suffice as it covers damages to third parties at a lower cost.

The premium depends on your car's cubic capacity (CC), market value, seating capacity, and whether you opt for add-ons like riot & strike damage, earthquake, or flood coverage. Comprehensive policies have higher premiums than liability-only policies.

Common exclusions include wear and tear, mechanical or electrical breakdown, damage while driving under the influence, using the vehicle for racing, and damage caused by war or nuclear risks. Always read your policy document carefully.

In case of an accident: 1) Ensure safety first, 2) File an FIR/GD at the nearest police station, 3) Notify UpShield Insurix within 24 hours, 4) Submit required documents including photos and repair estimates, 5) Our team will assess and process your claim promptly.

Claims are settled based on the assessed damage, policy coverage limits, and depreciation schedule. For total loss, the insured declared value minus depreciation is paid. For partial loss, actual repair costs are reimbursed subject to policy terms.
