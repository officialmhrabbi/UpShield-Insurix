Choose the best Health Insurance Plan

for Yourself and your Family

Health insurance is a type of insurance policy that provides financial coverage against illness, diseases, and accident-related treatments. That means you can get financial benefit against your medical expenses if you have a health insurance policy. All you have to do is buy a good health insurance policy and renew it every year because you never know when an accident may happen or you get sick.

UpShield Insurix offers various health insurance plans on its website and mobile app from where you can easily understand the terms and conditions and purchase online and get claims.

Medical treatment costs are rising every year. A single hospital stay can wipe out years of savings. Health insurance protects you from these unexpected expenses so you can focus on recovery instead of bills. It also covers preventive check-ups, prescription drugs, and emergency care depending on the plan you choose.

UpShield Insurix offers individual health plans, family floater plans, senior citizen plans, critical illness plans, and group health insurance from top insurers including Guardian Life, MetLife, GreenDelta, and Prudential. You can compare premiums, sum insured options, and benefits side by side before you buy.

An Individual Plan covers one person with a dedicated sum insured. A Family Floater Plan covers your whole family under one shared sum insured at a lower combined premium. Floaters work well for young families where claims are less frequent, while individual plans suit older members who may need higher dedicated coverage.

You can claim in two ways. Cashless: get treatment at a network hospital and the insurer pays the bill directly. Reimbursement: pay first, then submit the bills, discharge summary, and reports to UpShield Insurix. Our claims team forwards everything to the insurer and follows up until your payment is processed.

The insurer reviews your documents and approves the claim if everything is in order. Cashless claims are usually settled within hours. Reimbursement claims take 7 to 15 working days depending on the case. Your sum insured reduces by the claim amount and resets when you renew the policy for the next year.

You get plans from every major insurer in one place with side-by-side comparisons, plain-language explanations, and an instant digital policy. Renewals, claims, and doorstep document delivery are all handled for you free of cost. No agent visits, no hidden charges.
