A lot of countries around the world require travel insurance to visit or stay for foreign nationals. Countries which require travel insurance include the United Kingdom (UK), Turkey, Egypt, Oman, Qatar, Indonesia, Nepal, Philippines, Russia, Singapore, United Arab Emirates, Vietnam, Japan, etc., and all Schengen Countries.

When selecting a travel insurance policy, consider the destination country's requirements, duration of your trip, the coverage amount needed, and any specific activities you plan to engage in. Business travelers may need different coverage than holiday travelers. Compare plans from multiple insurers on UpShield Insurix to find the best fit for your needs and budget.

Business travel insurance typically covers trip cancellation, business equipment, and liability. Holiday plans focus on medical emergencies, trip interruption, lost baggage, and personal accident coverage. Both types provide 24/7 emergency assistance while abroad.

Travel insurance premiums depend on several factors: destination country, trip duration, traveler's age, coverage amount, and the type of plan selected. Premiums can range from a few hundred to several thousand taka. Use the UpShield Insurix platform to compare prices from multiple insurers instantly.

Common exclusions include pre-existing medical conditions, extreme sports or adventure activities (unless specifically covered), travel to sanctioned countries, self-inflicted injuries, and claims arising from substance abuse. Always read the policy document carefully before purchasing.

In case of a claim, contact the insurance company's helpline immediately.
